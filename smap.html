Inchican is a barangay in the municipality of Silang, in the province of Cavite. Its population as determined by the 2015 Census was 5,369. This represented 2.16% of the total population of Silang.

Population by age group

According to the 2015 Census, the age group with the highest population in Inchican is 5 to 9, with 568 individuals. Conversely, the age group with the lowest population is 80 and over, with 20 individuals.

Historical population

The population of Inchican grew from 767 in 1990 to 5,369 in 2015, an increase of 4,602 people. The latest census figures in 2015 denote a positive growth rate of 4.39%, or an increase of 1,085 people, from the previous population of 4,284 in 2010.
